Our Mission


Due to climate change and the ever-increasing home prices, there is always a need for supplies such as food, water, energy, and medicine. Homelessness and the number of fires in California are rising. Many people cannot afford basic needs and for that reason, we created Pudl, a way to request supplies quickly and efficiently; it is always there to help when you most need help. We soon hope to partner with other larger charities and companies who deliver their products as fast as possible. We value our customers above all and treat everyone as though they are our family.
